HR System

Bonanza human resources system:  It is an integrated system that aims to facilitate and improve human resources management processes within institutions and companies. The Bonanza system aims to organize and improve the performance of all aspects related to human resources in the organization, from recruiting employees to managing working hours and evaluating performance.
